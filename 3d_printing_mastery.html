Why you should attend this workshop ?

Tamil Nadu is rapidly emerging as a global hub for 3D Printing, Additive Manufacturing, and Rapid Prototyping — and this workshop is your gateway to enter that exciting world with confidence. Whether you're a student, hobbyist, tech enthusiast, this is the perfect opportunity to gain hands-on experience and industry-relevant skills.

What You'll Learn ?

To Explore Thriving 3D Printing Opportunities !

Discover the booming 3D printing industry in Tamil Nadu with opportunities in rapid prototyping, custom manufacturing, medical devices, and emerging technologies like bioprinting and aerospace applications.

Master 3D Printing Technologies

Learn about FDM, SLA, SLS printing technologies, filament materials, print settings, and quality control. Understand their applications, specifications, and practical uses in various industries.

Tamil Language Advantage

Learn 3D printing concepts in Tamil for better understanding and retention. Connect with local industry professionals and access regional job opportunities more effectively.

Empower Yourself for the Manufacturing Future !

Build skills for future technologies including custom manufacturing, rapid prototyping, medical implants, and Industry 4.0 applications. Prepare for careers in emerging manufacturing fields.

Hands-On 3D Printing Mastery !

Design and print real 3D objects, use CAD software and slicing tools, troubleshoot printing issues, and create working prototypes from concept sketches to finished products.

Introduction to CAD & 3D Modeling

Master CAD software like Fusion 360 and Tinkercad, create parametric designs, understand design for manufacturing principles, and develop 3D models for various applications.

Career Guidance & Industry Insights

Get insights into 3D printing career paths, industry trends, job market analysis, and networking opportunities. Learn about higher education options and professional certifications in additive manufacturing.
