About ELARCHIPHY 1.0

The perfect beginner's kit for exploring the foundational concepts of electronics. It comes with essential components to build and experiment with 10 hands-on, practical circuits that ignite curiosity and learning.

  • Gear Motors (x2)
  • Tyres (x2)
  • Caster Wheel
  • Motor Driver – DRV8833
  • Breadboard
  • Connecting Wires (1 batch)
  • LEDs
  • Resistors – 220Ω, 10kΩ
  • LDRs (Light Dependent Resistors)
  • IC 358 (Op-Amp)
  • IC 741 (Op-Amp)

  • Light Follower Robot – Follows light like a curious explorer
  • Comparator Circuit – Learn how decisions are made in robots
  • Line Follower Robot – Detect and follow black lines using sensors

  • Build your first autonomous robots

  • Understand robotic motion, path detection, and sensor logic
