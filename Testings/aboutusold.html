Our CEO & Founder

Gokul Kittusamy is the Founder and CEO of Elarchitek, a leading institution focused on innovation and excellence in technology. He holds a BE in Production Engineering from the prestigious Madras Institute of Technology, Chennai. With a passion for mechanical design and manufacturing engineering, Gokul drives practical solutions to real-world challenges.

Under his leadership, Elarchitek has become a forward-thinking organization advancing technology and education. He actively participates in community development, conducting hands-on education sessions in government schools to inspire young innovators. His entrepreneurial spirit and expertise in mechanical engineering have shaped Elarchitek’s growth and impact.

Gokul is dedicated to fostering inclusivity and knowledge-sharing. His volunteer work highlights his commitment to societal betterment. As a visionary leader, he continues to push the boundaries of innovation. Gokul’s journey inspires engineers, educators, and leaders in the tech industry.
