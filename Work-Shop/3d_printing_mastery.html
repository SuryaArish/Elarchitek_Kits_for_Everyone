Complete Learning Curriculum

When you join any 3D printing workshop, you will get these comprehensive modules designed to make you a 3D printing expert

3D Printer & Technology Basics

Master the fundamentals of 3D printing technology, understand how printers work, and learn essential safety practices.

You'll be able to:
  • Operate any FDM 3D printer safely
  • Understand printer components and functions
  • Follow proper safety protocols

Printer Settings & Operation

Learn to fine-tune your printer for perfect results. Master bed leveling, calibration, and troubleshooting techniques.

You'll be able to:
  • Set up and calibrate any 3D printer
  • Load filaments and adjust settings
  • Fix common printing problems

Tinkercad Design Basics

Start your design journey with Tinkercad. Create your first 3D models using simple, intuitive tools perfect for beginners.

You'll be able to:
  • Design simple 3D objects from scratch
  • Use basic modeling tools confidently
  • Export models ready for printing

Cura Slicer Mastery

Transform your 3D models into printable files. Learn to optimize settings for quality, speed, and material efficiency.

You'll be able to:
  • Slice models with optimal settings
  • Control print quality and speed
  • Generate perfect G-code files

Bambu Slicer Interface

Explore advanced slicing with Bambu Studio. Learn multi-material printing, remote monitoring, and professional workflows.

You'll be able to:
  • Use professional slicing software
  • Handle multi-color printing projects
  • Monitor prints remotely

Advanced Bambu Settings

Master professional-grade printing with advanced calibration, flow tuning, and diagnostic tools for perfect results.

You'll be able to:
  • Fine-tune printer performance
  • Calibrate for any filament type
  • Achieve professional print quality

Functional Design & Engineering

Create models that work in the real world. Learn about tolerances, part orientation, and design for manufacturing principles.

You'll be able to:
  • Design functional, working parts
  • Optimize designs for 3D printing
  • Create assemblies and moving parts

Quality Testing & Optimization

Perfect your printing skills with systematic testing. Learn to tune settings for different materials and achieve consistent results.

You'll be able to:
  • Test and optimize print settings
  • Achieve consistent, high-quality results
  • Troubleshoot any printing issues

Complete Workflow Mastery

Put it all together! Master the complete process from initial concept to finished product with quality control and finishing techniques.

You'll be able to:
  • Manage complete printing projects
  • Ensure consistent quality control
  • Deliver professional results

Blender for Creative Design

Unlock artistic 3D modeling with Blender. Create organic shapes, sculptures, and artistic models that stand out from the crowd.

You'll be able to:
  • Create artistic and organic models
  • Use professional sculpting tools
  • Design unique, creative objects

SolidWorks Engineering Basics

Enter the world of professional CAD design. Learn parametric modeling, constraints, and engineering design principles.

You'll be able to:
  • Create precise engineering drawings
  • Use parametric design principles
  • Design with professional CAD tools

Advanced SolidWorks & Simulation

Master professional engineering design with assemblies, simulations, and advanced modeling techniques used in industry.

You'll be able to:
  • Design complex mechanical assemblies
  • Run basic engineering simulations
  • Create industry-standard designs
