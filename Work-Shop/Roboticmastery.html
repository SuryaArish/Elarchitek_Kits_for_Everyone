Why you should attend this workshop ?

Tamil Nadu is rapidly emerging as a global hub for Robotics, Automation, and AI Development — and this workshop is your gateway to enter that exciting world with confidence. Whether you're a student, hobbyist, tech enthusiast, this is the perfect opportunity to gain hands-on experience and industry-relevant skills.

What You'll Learn ?

To Explore Thriving Robotics Opportunities !

Discover the booming robotics industry in Tamil Nadu with opportunities in industrial automation, service robotics, autonomous vehicles, and emerging technologies like AI-powered robots and smart manufacturing.

Master Robot Components & Systems

Learn about motors, sensors, actuators, microcontrollers, and communication systems. Understand their functions, specifications, and practical applications in robotic systems.

Tamil Language Advantage

Learn robotics concepts in Tamil for better understanding and retention. Connect with local industry professionals and access regional job opportunities more effectively.

Empower Yourself for the Robotic Future !

Build skills for future technologies including autonomous systems, AI robotics, industrial automation, and smart manufacturing. Prepare for careers in emerging robotics fields.

Hands-On Robotic Mastery !

Build real robots from scratch, program autonomous behaviors, integrate sensors and actuators, and create working robotic projects from line followers to intelligent navigation systems.

Introduction to Arduino & Robot Programming

Program Arduino microcontrollers for robotics, interface with various sensors (ultrasonic, gyroscope, camera), and create intelligent robotic behaviors with real-world applications.

Career Guidance & Industry Insights

Get insights into robotics career paths, industry trends, job market analysis, and networking opportunities. Learn about higher education options and professional certifications in robotics.
