Why you should attend this workshop ?

Tamil Nadu is rapidly emerging as a global hub for Electronics Design, Manufacturing, and Embedded System Development — and this workshop is your gateway to enter that exciting world with confidence. Whether you're a student, hobbyist, tech enthusiast, this is the perfect opportunity to gain hands-on experience and industry-relevant skills.

What You'll Learn ?

To Explore Thriving Opportunities !

Discover the booming electronics industry in Tamil Nadu with opportunities in semiconductor manufacturing, IoT development, automotive electronics, and emerging technologies like AI and robotics.

Master the Core Components

Learn about resistors, capacitors, inductors, diodes, transistors, and integrated circuits. Understand their functions, specifications, and practical applications in electronic circuits.

Tamil Language Advantage

Learn electronics concepts in Tamil for better understanding and retention. Connect with local industry professionals and access regional job opportunities more effectively.

Empower Yourself for the Future !

Build skills for future technologies including IoT, smart devices, renewable energy systems, and Industry 4.0 applications. Prepare for careers in emerging tech fields.

Hands-On Electronic Mastery !

Build real circuits on breadboards, use multimeters and oscilloscopes, solder components, and create working electronic projects from LED circuits to sensor-based systems.

Introduction to Arduino & Sensors

Program Arduino microcontrollers, interface with various sensors (temperature, light, motion), and create interactive electronic projects with real-world applications.

Career Guidance & Industry Insights

Get insights into electronics career paths, industry trends, job market analysis, and networking opportunities. Learn about higher education options and professional certifications.
