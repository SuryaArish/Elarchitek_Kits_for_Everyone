Mr. Gokul Kittusamy

Founder & Chief Executive Officer (CEO)

Gokul Kittusamy is the visionary Founder and Chief Executive Officer (CEO) of Elarchitek, a leading institution dedicated to fostering innovation and excellence in technology. With a strong academic background, he completed his B.E. in Production Engineering from the prestigious Madras Institute of Technology, Chennai.

His expertise in mechanical design and manufacturing engineering drives his commitment to bringing practical solutions to real-world challenges. As the Founder and CEO, Gokul leads Elarchitek with a sharp focus on innovation, growth, and community empowerment.

His expertise in mechanical engineering and design, combined with his leadership acumen, has steered Elarchitek into a forward-thinking organization making significant strides in the fields of technology, architecture, and education.
