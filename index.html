Introduction to 3D Printing

3D Printing & Modelling Workshop Join Elarchitek’s hands-on workshop and explore the complete journey from digital design to physical creation. Workshop Highlights: Design your own 3D models Understand the working of 3D printers Learn slicing, printing, and troubleshooting techniques Perfect for students, makers, and beginners looking to build real-world skills in 3D design and fabrication.
